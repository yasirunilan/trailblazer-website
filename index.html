INVENT YOURSELF

What is
TRAILBLAZER?

The objective of the Forum is to enhance a young individual’s leadership aspect of the life while inculcating timely change management along with effective life excellence tips.

The LEO Leadership Forum is the biggest public event the Leo Multiple District 306 is hosting in the fiscal year 2018/2019 focusing to gather young professionals from the corporate world.

LEO MULTIPLE DISTRICT 306

Fulfilling a great
Social Responsibility

The proceeds of the event would be donated to Apeksha Hospital Maharagama to ensure that the outcome of the event is invested in to make an impact to help enhance the lifestyle of the children patients.

A Life changing
opportunity

TRAILBLAZER is a strong individual that can pave and make a path to reach success and excellence easily and strategically, in midst of any challenge in its way! It is about a person who can make everything happen with having nothing! This platform is created for young professionals in Sri Lanka to gain knowledge and enhance their lifestyle and be a ‘LEADER’. In the opportunity given to you here, you can certainly grasp the experiences from the leading pace-setters in the country to absorb their knowledge and pour them in to your workplaces and lives to seize and conquer the challenges as a warrior! Change is something we are all afraid. Aren’t we? Let’s take this step as one, and learn how to embrace the change with the evolving technology around us to be an intrapreneur!
